Food Truck Commission Payment
Each year a limited set of food trucks is invited to set up at the Colonial Heritage Festival. They pay a commission to the Colonial Heritage Foundation based on their sales at the event. This page allows them remit that commission.
This button assumes you already have the Venmo app installed on your phone.
This button will let you pay with a credit card or with Apple Pay, even if you don't have a paypal account.
